KOZHIKOD

Basking in the idyllic setting of the serene Arabian Sea on the west and mesmerising peaks of the Wayanad hills on the east, this district has all the required ingredients to fascinate a tourist including a host of major attractions.Due to its nearness to the sea, the place has a hot and humid climate. EXPLORE THE DESTINATIONS



Kappad

Kappad is the place where Vasco da Gama set foot for the first time when he came to India on May 27, 1498. There is a small stone monument at the beach to commemorate that event.

Pazhassi rajah Museum

Pazhassi rajah Museum is a treasure house of ancient exhibits belonging to the rajas of bye gone days. Adjacent to it is an art gallery displaying paintings of Raja Ravi Varma.

Science center and Planetarium

Science center and Planetarium at Jaffarkhan Colony is the best place to unravel the mysteries of the universe and get enlightened about the planets and stars as seen from the place. This planetarium has an interesting array of games, puzzles, and scientific gadgetry, which can keep a person busyviewing for the whole day.

Mananchira

Mananchira which was the palace tank of King Mana Vikrama, has been now turned into a traditional architectural splendor with carpeted green grass and surrounded by ethnic buildings. The place also has a musical fountain and is worth visiting in Kozhikode.
